Frequently asked questions and answers

Which companies are suitable for Nexera Automation?

A brilliant question! Nexera Automation caters to entrepreneurs and small to medium sized businesses who are looking to bulletproof their business via AI. Our customer service chatbots can be seamlessly integrated into your business in just a few days.

Can I customize the chatbot to reflect my brand's personality and style?

Absolutely, we can customize the chatbot's response and appearance to match your brand identity and create a unique customer experience.

Can the chatbot be integrated with my existing systems and website?

Absolutely, our chatbot integrates smoothly with your existing systems, ensuring a hassle-free installation process.

What happens if the chatbot cannot answer a customer's question?

If the chatbot cannot help the customer, it will transfer the conversation to a human customer service agent who can assist the customer.

Will the chatbot reduce my customer service costs?

Yes, by automating routine tasks and requests, the chatbot can significantly reduce your customer support costs, allowing you to allocate resources more efficiently.

Does the chatbot keep up with the latest e-commerce trends and technologies?

Yes, we are continuously updating and improving our chatbot to incorporate the latest e-commerce trends and technologies to keep your business at the forefront.

How do you ensure that the chatbot does not give inappropriate answers?

The chatbot follows predefined guidelines and instructions to ensure it delivers consistent, high-quality interactions that align with your brand's values and standards.

Is there a refund policy if I am not satisfied with the service?

We do not charge any initial costs for the development of your chatbot. You can easily unsubscribe if you are dissatisfied.
